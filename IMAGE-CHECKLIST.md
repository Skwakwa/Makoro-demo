# Makoro Bricks & Tanks — Image Checklist
# Save ALL images inside the /images/ folder

## MUST HAVE (site won't look complete without these)

| File Name             | What to Ask For                              | Size Tip          |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| images/logo.png       | "Can I get your logo file?"                  | PNG, transparent  |
| images/hero.jpg       | Photo of yard, entrance or bricks stacked    | Wide/landscape    |
| images/about.jpg      | Storefront, staff, or owner photo            | Any               |

---

## BRICKS DIVISION

| File Name                  | What to Ask For                         |
|----------------------------|-----------------------------------------|

| images/brick-standard.jpg  | Close-up photo of a standard brick      |
| images/brick-maxi.jpg      | Close-up photo of a maxi brick          |
| images/block-hollow.jpg    | Photo of hollow concrete block          |
| images/block-solid.jpg     | Photo of solid concrete block           |

## TANKS DIVISION

| File Name             | What to Ask For                        | Notes              |
|-----------------------|----------------------------------------|--------------------|
| images/tank-250.png   | Photo of 250L tank                     | PNG preferred      |
| images/tank-500.png   | Photo of 500L tank                     | PNG preferred      |
| images/tank-2500.png  | Photo of 2500L tank                    | PNG preferred      |
| images/tank-5000.png  | Photo of 5000L tank                    | PNG preferred      |

---

## TIPS

- Ask them to WhatsApp you the photos — most will have them on their phone
- If no logo file exists, ask for a photo of their signage — you can recreate it
- PNG = better for tanks (no background)
- JPG = fine for everything else
- Run all photos through squoosh.app before uploading — keeps site fast
- If they dont have a photo for a product, search free stock on unsplash.com
